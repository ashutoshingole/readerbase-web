Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

This policy describes how Readerbase (“the app”) handles information. Readerbase is built as a local-first app: your library, notes, and reading data stay primarily on your device.

What we collect

  • On device: Books, shelves, notes, vault content, preferences, and similar data you create in the app are stored locally unless you explicitly use a feature that sends data elsewhere.
  • Diagnostics: If you use crash or analytics tools in the future, this page should list them here. Currently, describe what you actually ship (e.g. none, or Firebase, etc.).
  • Support: If you email us (e.g. feedback), we receive the content you send and your email address.

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell your personal library or reading data.
  • We do not require an account for core use of the app unless you add that feature later—update this section if that changes.
